Our Donate Plan

We plan to help the most vulnerable people to access nutritious food and essential items as soon as possible.

Food for a family

We supported the affected families with ready to eat food that could support a family of 5 for a week. The kit also included hygiene materials to ensure their safety during this flood.

Shelter for a family

Humanitarian General Purpose Tent System (HGPTS) is crafted to be put together quickly and easily to aid in response to natural disasters and emergencies. These shelters are equipped with roofs, walls, and flooring that are connected to form a securely enclosed structure.

Education for a family

Donating will allow children to have a scholarship to attend school every day in the best possible conditions and to access extra-curricular activities that will develop their self-confidence, social and cultural awareness as well as their innovation and entrepreneurial spirit to become accomplished leaders of tomorrow.
